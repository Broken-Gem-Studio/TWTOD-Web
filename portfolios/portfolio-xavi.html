Xavi Marín - Artist

I’m Xavi Marin, artist of The Witcher: Ties of Destiny and I’m really interested in 3D art and Level Design.
I think that when creating environments you must take into account that both asset creation and level design are essential elements for the game development process. That’s why I try to integrate both of these elements in order to create unique and special environments.

On one hand, I have realized it’s not an easy task, that coordinate with designers, maintain the same artstyle, fit everything to have a good overview of the scenario, etc. is difficult and not only requires 3D skills.
But on the other hand, I have also realized that this is what I want to do. I have really enjoyed the experience of creating this game with my 28 teammates that have done an incredible work!

PROJECT CONTRIBUTION

I couldn’t talk about my project contribution without mentioning Roger León and Joan Barduena:
- Roger León has been my lead and has helped me a lot. He has always gave me advice to improve my skills and my results.
- Joan Barduena and I have worked aside in different tasks and I can say that we formed a very good duo. It has been a pleasure working with him!
During the project I have worked mainly as environment artist but I have also done some art stuff for UI:

  • Study of environments: This tasks involve creating a list of possible assets that can contain, make a study of light, decide the color palette of the scenario and do a matte painting to represent it.
  • Materials and Textures: I have learnt Substance Designer in order to create new textures and optimize the polycount of some assets.
  • Props: Creation of some environments props (foliage, lamp, bottles, rocks…).
  • Game Baked Textures Stuff (Working with Joan Barduena): We baked some textures and tested how was the performance with 4K and 2K textures. Then, with all the baked textures done, we corrected those textures that had render errors.
  • Game Logo (Working with Rafel Brau): I designed and draw the logo of the game. Then, Rafel Brau painted.
  • UI:
    - Combos Menu: Design and creation.
    - "Big Instructions Cards" of the tutorial: Design and creation.
    - "Little Instructions Cards" of the tutorial: Design and creation.
  • Web design and creation (Working with Joan Barduena): We were in charge of creating the website. To do this, we have learnt HTML and CSS in few days to have a very good-looking web.
